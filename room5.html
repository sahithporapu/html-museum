🏛 Details and Summary

What is <details>?

The <details> tag is used to hide some content until the user chooses to see it. It creates a collapsible box that the user can click to expand or collapse.

What is <summary>?

The <summary> tag is placed inside <details>. It shows the title or question — and when clicked, it will show the hidden content inside <details>.



🕵️ Hidden HTML Facts

🤫 Did You Know?

The <details> and <summary> tags were introduced in HTML5 to help show or hide content easily.


📖 HTML Was Created In?

🔍 What Is <em> Tag?

The <em> tag is used to emphasize text, usually displayed in italics.
